About Our Jewelry

Yaniv Fine Jewelry, a multigenerational family business, was established in the 1950s by Yosef and Aviva Yaniv.

In 1982, Shai Yaniv took the reins and opened “The Mythical Studio,” one of the first of its kind in Tel Aviv.

One of the unique aspects of Yaniv Fine Jewelry is the studio and workshop on Ben Yehuda Street in Tel Aviv.
